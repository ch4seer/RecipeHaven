Milkshakes & Frozen Drinks

Chocolate Milkshake

Ingredients: 2 cups vanilla ice cream, 1 cup milk, 3 tbsp chocolate syrup, whipped cream

Instructions:

  1. Blend vanilla ice cream, milk, and chocolate syrup until smooth.
  2. Pour into a glass and top with whipped cream.
  3. Serve immediately with a straw and enjoy!

Frozen Strawberry Lemonade

Ingredients: 1 cup strawberries, 1/2 cup lemon juice, 2 tbsp sugar, 1 cup ice

Instructions:

  1. Blend strawberries, lemon juice, sugar, and ice until smooth.
  2. Serve in a chilled glass and garnish with a lemon slice.
  3. Enjoy this refreshing frozen drink!

Banana Milkshake

Ingredients: 1 banana, 2 cups vanilla ice cream, 1 cup milk, honey (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Blend banana, vanilla ice cream, milk, and honey until smooth.
  2. Serve in a tall glass with a straw and enjoy!

Tropical Frozen Drink

Ingredients: 1/2 cup mango, 1/2 cup pineapple, 1/2 cup coconut milk, 1 cup ice

Instructions:

  1. Blend mango, pineapple, coconut milk, and ice until smooth.
  2. Serve chilled in a tropical-themed glass and enjoy!
