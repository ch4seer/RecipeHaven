Hot Beverages

Espresso

Ingredients: 1 shot of espresso

Instructions:

  1. Grind fresh coffee beans and pack them into the espresso machine.
  2. Pull a shot of espresso and serve immediately in a small cup.
  3. Enjoy your strong and aromatic espresso!

Hot Chocolate

Ingredients: 2 cups milk, 2 tbsp cocoa powder, 2 tbsp sugar, whipped cream (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Heat the milk in a pot until warm, but not boiling.
  2. Whisk in cocoa powder and sugar until fully dissolved.
  3. Pour into a mug and top with whipped cream, if desired.
  4. Serve hot and enjoy!

Cappuccino

Ingredients: 1 shot espresso, 1/2 cup steamed milk, 1/2 cup frothed milk

Instructions:

  1. Prepare a shot of espresso and pour it into a cup.
  2. Steam milk and froth it to a creamy consistency.
  3. Pour steamed milk over the espresso and top with frothed milk.
  4. Garnish with cocoa powder or cinnamon, if desired.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Chai Latte

Ingredients: 1 cup milk, 1/2 cup brewed chai tea, 1 tbsp honey or sugar

Instructions:

  1. Brew chai tea and add honey or sugar for sweetness.
  2. Heat milk until warm, but not boiling.
  3. Pour chai tea into a cup and add warm milk.
  4. Stir and garnish with a sprinkle of cinnamon or nutmeg.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!
