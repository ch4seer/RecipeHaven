Indian Cuisine

Curry

Ingredients:

  • Chicken or lamb
  • Curry paste
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Garlic, ginger, and spices

Instructions:

  1. Fry onions, garlic, and ginger in oil.
  2. Add curry paste, tomatoes, and meat.
  3. Simmer until meat is tender, garnish with cilantro.

Biryani

Ingredients:

  • Basmati rice
  • Meat (chicken, mutton, or beef)
  • Yogurt
  • Spices (cumin, cloves, cinnamon)
  • Onions, garlic, and ginger

Instructions:

  1. Cook the meat with yogurt and spices.
  2. Layer rice and cooked meat, cook on low heat.
  3. Garnish with fried onions and serve.

Naan

Ingredients:

  • All-purpose flour
  • Yogurt
  • Baking powder
  • Garlic, butter

Instructions:

  1. Mix flour, yogurt, and baking powder to form dough.
  2. Roll out the dough, and cook on a hot griddle or in the oven.
  3. Brush with melted butter and serve warm.

Samosa

Ingredients:

  • Potatoes
  • Onions
  • Peas
  • Spices (cumin, coriander, turmeric)
  • Spring roll wrappers

Instructions:

  1. Cook potatoes and peas with spices.
  2. Fill spring roll wrappers with the mixture and deep fry.
  3. Serve with chutney.
