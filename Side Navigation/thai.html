Thai Cuisine

Pad Thai

Ingredients:

  • Rice noodles
  • Shrimp
  • Peanuts
  • Eggs
  • Bean sprouts
  • Fish sauce, tamarind paste, sugar

Instructions:

  1. Cook the noodles and shrimp.
  2. Stir-fry with eggs, peanuts, and bean sprouts.
  3. Season with fish sauce, tamarind paste, and sugar.

Green Curry

Ingredients:

  • Green curry paste
  • Coconut milk
  • Chicken or tofu
  • Eggplant
  • Basil leaves

Instructions:

  1. Cook the curry paste in a pan with coconut milk.
  2. Add chicken or tofu, and cook until tender.
  3. Add eggplant and simmer. Garnish with basil leaves.

Tom Yum Soup

Ingredients:

  • Shrimp
  • Lemongrass
  • Galangal
  • Kaffir lime leaves
  • Chili paste

Instructions:

  1. Boil shrimp, lemongrass, galangal, and lime leaves in water.
  2. Add chili paste and fish sauce, then simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. Serve hot with fresh cilantro and lime juice.

Thai Salad

Ingredients:

  • Mixed greens
  • Cucumber
  • Carrots
  • Peanuts
  • Fish sauce, lime juice, sugar

Instructions:

  1. Mix the greens, cucumber, and carrots.
  2. Top with peanuts and season with fish sauce, lime juice, and sugar.
