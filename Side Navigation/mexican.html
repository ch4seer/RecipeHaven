Mexican Cuisine

Tacos

Ingredients:

  • 8 small tortillas
  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup shredded cheese
  • Optional: sour cream and guacamole

Instructions:

  1. Cook ground beef in a skillet over medium heat until browned.
  2. Add taco seasoning and a small amount of water; simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. Warm tortillas in a pan or microwave.
  4. Assemble tacos with beef, lettuce, tomatoes, and cheese.
  5. Top with sour cream and guacamole if desired. Serve warm.

Burritos

Ingredients:

  • 4 large tortillas
  • 500g cooked chicken or beef
  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 1 cup refried beans
  • 1 cup shredded cheese
  • 1/2 cup salsa
  • Optional: sour cream and guacamole

Instructions:

  1. Warm tortillas in a pan or microwave.
  2. Layer rice, beans, meat, cheese, and salsa on each tortilla.
  3. Fold the sides of the tortilla inward, then roll it up tightly.
  4. Optional: grill the burritos in a pan for a crispy exterior. Serve warm.

Quesadillas

Ingredients:

  • 4 large tortillas
  • 2 cups shredded cheese
  • 1 cup cooked chicken or vegetables
  • 1/2 cup salsa
  • Optional: sour cream and guacamole

Instructions:

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Place a tortilla in the skillet and sprinkle half with cheese and fillings.
  3. Fold the tortilla in half and cook until golden brown on each side.
  4. Cut into wedges and serve with salsa, sour cream, or guacamole.

Nachos

Ingredients:

  • 1 large bag tortilla chips
  • 2 cups shredded cheese
  • 1 cup cooked ground beef or beans
  • 1/2 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup sliced jalapeños
  • Optional: sour cream and guacamole

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C (400°F).
  2. Spread tortilla chips on a large baking sheet.
  3. Top with cheese, meat or beans, tomatoes, and jalapeños.
  4. Bake for 5-7 minutes until cheese is melted.
  5. Serve with sour cream and guacamole.
