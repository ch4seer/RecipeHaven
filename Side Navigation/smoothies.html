Smoothies & Juices

Green Smoothie

Ingredients: 1 cup spinach, 1/2 apple, 1/2 banana, 1/2 cup almond milk, ice cubes

Instructions:

  1. Place all ingredients into a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth, adding more almond milk if needed.
  3. Serve chilled with extra ice cubes if desired.

Carrot Orange Juice

Ingredients: 2 large carrots, 1 orange, 1/2 lemon, ice cubes

Instructions:

  1. Peel and chop the carrots, orange, and lemon.
  2. Blend all ingredients together with ice cubes.
  3. Strain the juice if desired, then serve fresh.

Mango Pineapple Smoothie

Ingredients: 1/2 mango, 1/2 cup pineapple chunks, 1/2 cup coconut milk, ice cubes

Instructions:

  1. Add mango, pineapple, coconut milk, and ice to a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth, adding more coconut milk if necessary.
  3. Serve chilled with a mint garnish.

Watermelon Juice

Ingredients: 2 cups watermelon, 1/2 lime, mint leaves, ice cubes

Instructions:

  1. Blend watermelon, lime juice, and ice cubes until smooth.
  2. Pour into a glass and garnish with mint leaves.
  3. Serve immediately for the best flavor.
